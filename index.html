about.

I'm curious about the world of how mechatronics intersect with inclusive design and interactivity.

I'm Joanna Cai, an undergraduate engineering student from the University of British Columbia, born and raised in Vancouver B.C. I hope to improve accessibility for inclusive learning around the globe radically. With my specialized skillset I'm working towards building a new world of interactive installations to Canada.
In 2024, I interned with Nokia, learning the basics of pytest and schemathesis with Nokia's notifier API in a corporate setting.
Combining my passions of music, engineering, and a curiosity for neurodiversity advocacy, I created Melokey.
